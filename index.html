About me

Software Engineer - Data ​Engineer

Aspiring Computer Science student at the University of Central Missouri, specializing in Data Engineering and Data Science. I bring hands-on experience as a Software Engineer – Data Engineer, where I developed scalable data pipelines and analytics solutions. My passion lies in transforming complex datasets into actionable insights, and I’m driven to leverage technology to solve real-world problems.

I’m Hemanth Kumar Sharab, a software engineer specializing in data science, data engineering, and full-stack development. I hold a B.Tech in Computer Science from Vel Tech Rangarajan Dr. Sagunthala R&D Institute, Chennai, and an M.S. in Computer Science from the University of Central Missouri, Lee’s Summit.

With a strong foundation in building data-driven solutions and user-friendly applications, I’ve contributed to impactful projects at Harman India, such as designing centralized data warehouses, scalable ETL pipelines, and optimizing data pipelines to enhance performance and reduce latency. My work has streamlined workflows, delivered actionable insights, and improved decision-making processes.

My expertise includes implementing machine learning models, creating advanced visual analytics dashboards, and building intuitive user interfaces integrated with backend APIs for accessible data insights. Technically, I’m skilled in Java, Python, R, C++, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, SQL, PySpark, TensorFlow, PyTorch, and more.

Over three years, I’ve gained hands-on experience in data science and machine learning, building end-to-end pipelines and exploring advanced analytical techniques. My passion lies in transforming raw data into actionable insights, and I’m eager to collaborate on innovative solutions.
